## Application Sections Summary

### 1. Project Overview

**Activity Type**: 3-day international workshop

**Key Information**:
- Location: American University of Sharjah, UAE
- Dates: April 21-23, 2026
- Participants: 80-100 (60% academic, 40% industry)
- Disciplines: AI/ML, Finance, Mathematics, Regulatory

**Character Count**: ~5,000 (within limit)

### 2. Aims and Objectives

**Transformative Vision**:
Catalyze fundamental transformation in Swiss-MENA collaboration on AI-driven financial innovation.

**Core Objectives**:
1. Establish Swiss-MENA AI Finance Research Network (MoU signing)
2. Create capacity building through knowledge transfer
3. Bridge academic research with industry implementation

**Expected Outcomes**:
- 10+ institutional MoU signatures
- 5-year research roadmap
- 3-5 joint research proposals
- 5+ joint publications within 12 months
- 2-3 formal industry-academia partnerships

**Character Count**: ~4,965 (within limit)

### 3. Format and Organization

**Three-Day Structure**:

| Day | Theme | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 1 | Research Frontiers | Academic presentations, keynotes |
| 2 | Industry Applications | Case studies, regulatory perspectives |
| 3 | Network Launch | Training, MoU signing, working groups |

**Format Features**:
- Hybrid delivery (in-person + streaming)
- Interactive sessions
- Structured networking
- Poster sessions for early-career researchers

### 4. Partnership and Roles

**Swiss Partner (FHGR)**:
- Lead applicant
- Academic program coordination
- International speaker recruitment
- CCG administration
- Proceedings publication

**MENA Partner (AUS)**:
- Local host
- Venue and equipment (in-kind)
- UAE speaker/industry connections
- Registration management
- Local logistics

**Complementary Strengths**:
- Switzerland: Financial technology expertise, regulatory frameworks
- MENA: Market dynamics, regulatory sandboxes, rapid digital adoption

### 5. Timeline and Risk Assessment

**Key Milestones**:
- Dec 2024: Application submission
- Feb 2025: Expected approval
- Apr 21-23, 2026: Workshop execution
- Jul 2026: Final reporting

**Risk Mitigation**:
- Hybrid format for travel restrictions
- Diverse program for multiple audiences
- Strong institutional backing
- Experienced organizing team
