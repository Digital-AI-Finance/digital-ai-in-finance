# Research Network

## Swiss-MENA AI Finance Research Network

![Network Map](images/network_map.png)

The Swiss-MENA AI Finance Research Network connects leading institutions across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia, creating a collaborative ecosystem for advancing AI applications in financial services.

## Collaboration Types

### Research Collaboration
Joint publications, shared methodologies, collaborative data analysis

**Active Between:** All partners

### Doctoral Co-supervision
Joint PhD supervision, doctoral exchanges, thesis committees

**Active Between:** FHGR-AUS, FHGR-Manchester, FHGR-Bern

### Industry Engagement
Joint industry projects, regulatory consultations, technology transfer

**Active Between:** Core partners (FHGR-AUS) with regional industry

### Grant Applications
Collaborative funding proposals, research program development

**Planned:** All partners for Horizon Europe, SNSF bilateral programs
