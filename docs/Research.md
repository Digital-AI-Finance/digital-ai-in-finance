## Research Areas

### 1. Cryptocurrency Markets & Statistical Analysis

The foundational research began in 2017 with comprehensive statistical analysis of cryptocurrency markets, establishing methodological frameworks that have been widely cited.

**Key Contributions:**
- GARCH modeling adapted for cryptocurrency volatility
- Statistical distribution analysis (Normal-Inverse Gaussian)
- Econometric approaches to digital asset valuation
- Market microstructure analysis

**Publications:** Chan et al. (2017a, 2017b)

### 2. Blockchain Security & Fraud Detection

Recent work focuses on security challenges in blockchain networks, developing AI-based approaches for anomaly detection and fraud prevention.

**Key Contributions:**
- Machine learning for blockchain fraud detection
- Anomaly detection in DeFi protocols
- Smart contract vulnerability analysis
- Privacy-preserving detection techniques

**Publications:** Osterrieder et al. (2023, 2024)

### 3. NFTs & Metaverse Economics

Emerging research on non-fungible tokens and metaverse economics, analyzing market dynamics and stylized facts of digital assets.

**Key Contributions:**
- Statistical properties of NFT markets
- Metaverse asset valuation
- Market efficiency analysis
- Cross-platform comparisons

**Publications:** Chan et al. (2024), Osterrieder et al. (2024)
