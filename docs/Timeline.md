## Critical Path Items

The following tasks are on the critical path and require careful monitoring:

1. **CCG Review Period** (Dec 2024 - Feb 2025)
   - Dependent: All subsequent activities
   - Risk: Delayed approval

2. **Call for Papers Launch** (Feb 2025)
   - Dependent: Paper submissions, final program
   - Risk: Insufficient submissions

3. **Paper Review Process** (Sep 2025)
   - Dependent: Final program development
   - Risk: Reviewer availability

4. **Final Program Development** (Oct-Nov 2025)
   - Dependent: All Day 1-2 sessions
   - Risk: Speaker confirmations

5. **Speaker Invitations** (Feb 2025 - Mar 2026)
   - Dependent: Keynotes, panels
   - Risk: High-profile speaker availability

6. **Registration Drive** (Jan-Apr 2026)
   - Dependent: Workshop viability
   - Risk: Under-registration

7. **Technical Setup** (Mar-Apr 2026)
   - Dependent: Hybrid format delivery
   - Risk: AV equipment issues

8. **Final Preparations** (Apr 2026)
   - Dependent: Successful execution
   - Risk: Last-minute issues
