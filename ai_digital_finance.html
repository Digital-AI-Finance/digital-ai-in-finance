Project Overview

This three-day workshop brings together researchers from Switzerland and the MENA region to explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in financial services. The event establishes a long-term collaborative platform between Swiss and UAE institutions, with FHGR and AUS as core partners.

The workshop addresses critical challenges and opportunities at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and financial technology, including large language models, explainable AI for compliance, blockchain security, and digital banking innovation.

Workshop Topics

The workshop welcomes contributions across six key thematic areas at the intersection of artificial intelligence and financial services.

Large Language Models in Finance

Applications of GPT, BERT, and domain-specific LLMs for financial text analysis, sentiment extraction, and automated reporting.

Explainable AI for Compliance

Interpretable ML models for regulatory compliance, audit trails, and transparent decision-making in financial services.

Blockchain Security & Fraud Detection

Machine learning approaches for cryptocurrency fraud detection, smart contract analysis, and blockchain forensics.

Machine Learning for Risk Management

Advanced ML techniques for credit risk, market risk, operational risk assessment and portfolio optimization.

Digital Banking Innovation

AI-powered digital banking services, robo-advisors, personalized financial products, and customer analytics.

Alternative Data in Finance

Non-traditional data sources including satellite imagery, social media, web scraping for financial predictions.
