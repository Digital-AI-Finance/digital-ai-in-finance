Dear Leading House MENA Committee,

On behalf of the American University of Sharjah (AUS), I am pleased to confirm our strong commitment to co-organizing and hosting the workshop "AI for Digital Finance: Launching a Swiss-MENA Research Network" in collaboration with Prof. Joerg Osterrieder from the University of Applied Sciences of the Grisons (FHGR), Switzerland.

## Partnership Background

AUS and FHGR have established a productive research collaboration over the past 7 years, during which Prof. Osterrieder conducted extended research collaboration at our Department of Mathematics. This partnership has yielded four significant publications on blockchain security, fraud detection, and digital assets in virtual environments, demonstrating our shared commitment to advancing AI applications in digital finance.

## Commitment to Workshop Support

AUS commits to providing the following support for the workshop:

### Direct Financial Contribution (CHF 5,000 equivalent)
- Venue provision: Conference facilities for 3 days (CHF 1,500 value)
- Technical equipment: Audio-visual and recording equipment (CHF 800 value)
- Catering support: Partial coverage of lunches and reception (CHF 2,300 value)
- Materials support: Workshop materials and supplies (CHF 400 value)

### In-Kind Contributions
- Prof. Stephen Chan's time for workshop organization and academic leadership
- Administrative staff support for event coordination
- IT infrastructure and technical support for hybrid event delivery
- Marketing and communication through AUS channels
- Access to our network of UAE financial institutions and regulatory bodies
- Student volunteers for event support

### Additional Support
- Assistance with visa facilitation for international participants
- Airport transfer coordination for keynote speakers
- Local transportation arrangements
- Access to AUS library and research facilities

## Strategic Alignment

This workshop aligns perfectly with AUS's strategic priorities in:
- Fostering international research collaborations
- Advancing AI and digital transformation research
- Strengthening industry-academia partnerships in the UAE
- Contributing to the UAE's vision for financial innovation

We view this workshop as a cornerstone for establishing a long-term Swiss-MENA research network in AI and digital finance, with potential for future bilateral research programs and joint funding applications.

## Confirmation

I hereby confirm that AUS will fulfill all commitments outlined above and will work closely with Prof. Osterrieder and the FHGR team to ensure the workshop's success.

We look forward to hosting this important international event and continuing our productive collaboration with Swiss research institutions.
